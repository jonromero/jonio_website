## VAIM 2021: "V as in Metaverse" - The Beginning

The first conference happened in Athens, Greece during the summer of 2021. I organized it in **30 days** while vacationing in Greece (yes, I destroyed my vacation).

### What Made It Special:
- **NFTs as Tickets**: Front-running Gary Vee, I built my own NFT ticketing system. Every attendee had to buy an NFT and use a crypto wallet - practicing what we preach.
- **Fireside Chats**: No boring podiums. All presentations were "firechats" - conversational interviews that kept the audience engaged for 6 hours straight. On a Saturday. In Greece. During summer.
- **Hackathon**: Gave away â‚¬2,500 (~$3,000) to participants. Some teams traveled 6 hours by train from Thessaloniki.
- **COVID-19 Compliant**: Strict testing requirements, masks, max 50 people per space.
- **3,200+ Subscribers** in 2 weeks through authentic content and non-stop posting.

### The Reality:
Not easy at all. Crazy stressed. Skipped eating during the conference. But incredibly gratifying. The 29 days before were brutal, but the event itself was worth it.

**Key Lesson**: Don't overthink it. Built to fail has never failed me.

## Why I Keep Doing This

Every year, I'm reminded why this conference matters:

1. **Community**: The people who show up want to build cool stuff and help each other succeed.
2. **Learning**: Each conference teaches me something new about organizing, community building, and what matters.
3. **Impact**: Creating opportunities for startups, connecting builders with investors, and bringing world-class knowledge to Athens.
4. **Fun**: Despite the stress, it's incredibly gratifying to be around friends and intelligent people in my favorite place in the world.
